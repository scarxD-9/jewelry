Silver Dress Watch

A silver dress watch is sleek, minimal, and elegant. It usually has a thin silver case, a simple dial (often white or black), and either a leather strap or a refined metal bracelet. These watches are designed to be worn with formal or business attire and focus more on style than extra features.

Silver Chronograph Watch

This type combines a silver case with sporty functionality. A silver chronograph watch includes multiple sub-dials and pushers on the side that allow it to act as a stopwatch. It’s popular for people who want a bold, eye-catching watch that works for both casual wear and active use.

Dive Watch

A silver dive watch is built for durability and water resistance. It typically has a stainless-silver case, a rotating bezel, luminous hands and markers, and a sturdy bracelet. While designed for underwater use, it’s also worn as a rugged everyday watch thanks to its strong, masculine look.
