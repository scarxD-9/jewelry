Rolex Day-Date (18k Gold)

A true icon in luxury watches, the Rolex Day-Date is often nicknamed the “President’s Watch.” First launched in 1956, it was the first wristwatch to display both the day of the week (spelled out) and the date — a technical milestone that endures today. Rolex exclusively offers this model in solid precious metals like 18k yellow gold, 18k white gold, and 18k Everose (rose gold), paired with the signature President bracelet that combines prestige with comfort.
